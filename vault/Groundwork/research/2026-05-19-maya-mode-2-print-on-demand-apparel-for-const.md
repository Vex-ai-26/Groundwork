## Revenue Potential

**POD Margins:**
- 
Custom and bespoke work delivers the highest gross margins, often exceeding 60%, because customers pay significantly more for personalization and unique designs

- 
Custom t-shirt prices can vary widely, with $20-$40 per shirt being the norm for high-quality prints. However, production costs, including t-shirts, printing, packaging, and labor, usually range from $5 to $15 per shirt. This gives you a profit margin of 50-70%

- 
Here's the 2026 benchmarks: 60-70% gross, 20-30% operating, 10-20% net margin (based on 600+ stores)


**Revenue Model:**
- **Low-volume starter:** 100 shirts/month × $15 margin = $1,500/month ($18K/year)
- **Mid-scale:** 500 shirts/month × $15 margin = $7,500/month ($90K/year)
- **With subscriber base:** 1,000 active subs × 20% conversion × $35 avg order = $7,000/month ($84K/year)

**Key advantage:** Zero inventory risk with POD. You're not Carhartt - you're selling identity, not utility workwear.
