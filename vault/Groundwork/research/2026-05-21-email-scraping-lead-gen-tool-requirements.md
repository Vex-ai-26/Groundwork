# Email Scraping & Lead Gen Tool Requirements

## Current Gap
No one on the Groundwork team (Maya, Iris, Rex, Curt) has email scraping or lead extraction capability.

## What We'd Need

### Option 1: Third-Party Tool
- **Services**: Hunter.io, Apollo.io, Snov.io, ZoomInfo
- **Capabilities**: Domain search, LinkedIn scraping, email verification, bulk export
- **Cost**: $50–200/mo depending on volume
- **Pros**: Plug-and-play, compliance built in, verified contacts
- **Cons**: Recurring cost, rate limits, data accuracy varies

### Option 2: Custom Script/Contractor
- **Tools**: Python + BeautifulSoup, Scrapy, Selenium for dynamic sites
- **Capabilities**: Custom scraping logic, niche targeting, proprietary source parsing
- **Cost**: $500–2k one-time build, hosting ~$20/mo
- **Pros**: Fully customized, no per-contact fees
- **Cons**: Maintenance, legal risk (CAN-SPAM, GDPR), data quality control

### Option 3: VA + Manual Research
- **Process**: Virtual assistant manually compiles contact lists from directories, LinkedIn, company sites
- **Cost**: $5–15/hr
- **Pros**: High accuracy, human judgment, flexible
- **Cons**: Slow, not scalable, labor-intensive

## Recommended Approach
If this is for **Groundwork** (construction education): Start with LinkedIn + industry directories + manual VA work. Low volume, high intent.

If this is for **Etsy side hustles** (wedding templates, etc.): Not applicable—Etsy drives inbound traffic; no cold outreach needed.

## Next Steps
1. Define use case: Who are we contacting and why?
2. Estimate volume: 100 leads/month or 10,000?
3. Budget: One-time or recurring spend?
4. Compliance: Do we have legal copy and opt-out handling?
