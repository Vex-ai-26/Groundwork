## Features Customers Expect

### **Monthly Budget Template:**

Tabs for income, expenses, budget targets, and dashboard for visual summary of spending; additional tabs for historical data and spending-to-budget comparison



Separate sections for income, expenses, and savings; graphical representations of spending categories; monthly summary comparing to previous months


### **Debt Payoff Tracker:**

List all creditors, balances, interest rates, minimum payments, payment dates, remaining balances



Debt snowball method: paying minimum on all debts while directing extra to smallest balance first; roll payment into next smallest debt



Avalanche method: pay highest interest rate first regardless of balance


### **Savings Goal Tracker:**

Enter goal name, target amount, target date; supports multiple goals; record monthly deposits with automatic progress updates



Summary showing total target across goals, total saved, total remaining, overall progress percentage; goal list with status column updating automatically



Categories column to group goals (Safety Net, Travel, Tech, Life Event, Housing, Transport)


### **Expense Tracker:**

Four columns minimum: Date, Vendor, Amount, Category; fifth column for Business Purpose



Categories for housing, food, transport, entertainment, health, subscriptions; date and amount tracking; payment method recording; notes for context


### **Yearly Budget:**

Comprehensive annual view across all 12 months; monthly breakdowns for detailed tracking; forecasting features for future planning


## SEO Title and Tag Formulas

**Etsy allows 140 characters for titles**, but 
only first 40-50 characters display in search results before truncation
.

**Title formula:**

Front-load primary keyword in first 40 characters - Etsy weights early title keywords more heavily



"Commas help both readability and SEO - they signal distinct phrases to algorithm while making title scannable"


**Best practice structure:**
`[Primary Keyword], [Platform], [Feature 1], [Feature 2], [Use Case], [Format]`

Example: `Monthly Budget Spreadsheet, Google Sheets Excel Template, Paycheck Budget Tracker, Debt Tracker, Bill Calendar`

**Tags strategy:**

Always use all 13 Etsy tags with 2-4 word buyer phrases - every empty tag slot is a missed search query



Target one high-quality keyword (2-3 words long) for each listing; include in product title, first line of description, and tags



Beginners should target keywords with competition less than 2,000 on eRank

## Prompts for Building Each Type

### **Monthly Budget Planner Prompt:**
"Create a monthly budget spreadsheet with the following tabs: (1) Dashboard showing income vs expenses with visual charts, (2) Income tracker with multiple income source fields, (3) Expense categories including housing, transportation, food, entertainment, health, utilities, (4) Budget vs Actual comparison columns, (5) Month-over-month summary. Include dropdown menus for categories, automatic calculations for totals and remaining budget, and conditional formatting to highlight overspending in red."

### **Debt Payoff Tracker Prompt:**
"Build a debt payoff calculator with: (1) Input sheet for listing all debts (creditor name, balance, interest rate, minimum payment), (2) Snowball method calculator ordering debts smallest to largest, (3) Avalanche method calculator ordering by highest interest first, (4) Payment schedule showing monthly payments and remaining balance, (5) Progress visualization chart, (6) Payoff date calculator, (7) Total interest paid comparison. Include formulas for PMT calculations and conditional formatting for completed debts."

### **Savings Goal Tracker Prompt:**
"Design a savings goal tracker with: (1) Goal setup section (goal name, target amount, target date, category), (2) Contribution log with date and amount fields, (3) Automatic progress calculation showing percentage complete, (4) Visual progress bars for each goal, (5) Summary dashboard showing total saved across all goals, (6) Status indicators (On Track, Behind, Completed), (7) Monthly contribution required calculator. Support up to 10 simultaneous goals. Include categories: Emergency Fund, Vacation, Home, Vehicle, Education."

### **Expense Tracker Prompt:**
"Create an expense tracking template with: (1) Transaction log with columns for Date, Description, Category, Amount, Payment Method, Notes, (2) Automatic monthly totals by category, (3) Spending by category pie chart, (4) Monthly trend line graph, (5) Budget vs actual variance report, (6) Category dropdown list including: Housing, Transportation, Food/Groceries, Dining Out, Entertainment, Healthcare, Utilities, Insurance, Debt Payments, Savings, Personal Care, Subscriptions, Shopping, Other. Include data validation and conditional formatting."
