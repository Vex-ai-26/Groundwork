## FIRST 3 LINES REWRITTEN

**Line 1:** Stop guessing where your money actually lives—this net worth tracker shows your complete financial picture in one spreadsheet.

**Line 2:** Track every asset (retirement accounts, property, vehicles) and liability (mortgages, student loans, credit cards) with automatic calculations and monthly snapshots.

**Line 3:** Watch your wealth trend over time with built-in charts—works in Excel or Google Sheets, no finance degree required.

## WHAT CHANGED

**Title:** Added "Tracker Spreadsheet" (higher search volume), specified platforms, included "Financial Planning" (broader net)

**Tags:** Swapped vague terms for search-verified phrases. Added "printable" (Etsy buyers love this), "budget planner" (related search), platform names

**Copy:** Led with the *problem* (scattered finances), not the feature list. Used conversational specificity ("Stop guessing" vs. "Complete template"). Removed jargon, added reassurance ("no finance degree required")

**Core shift:** From describing WHAT it is → to solving WHY someone searches at 11pm Sunday realizing they have no idea what they're worth
