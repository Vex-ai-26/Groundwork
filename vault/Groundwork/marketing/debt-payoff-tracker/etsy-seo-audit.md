## WHAT CHANGED

**Title:** Added "planner," "spreadsheet," "debt free," and "payment schedule"—all high-volume searches. Specified file type (Excel) upfront.

**Tags:** Swapped generic terms for actual search phrases. Mixed method names (snowball/avalanche), formats (spreadsheet/excel), and use cases (budget planner, money management).

**Description:** 
- Opens with the *problem* (debt stress) not the product
- Shows outcome first (save money, know what to do)
- Confirms compatibility early (Excel/Sheets)
- Uses bullet points for scanability
- Speaks like a friend who's been there, not a finance bro

The old version assumed people knew what they needed. The new version meets them in their frustration and guides them to the solution.
