## What Changed

**From feature-list to benefit-first**: Original reads like spec sheet. Revision opens with the outcome (visual tracking, automatic calculations doing the work FOR you).

**Search-term injection**: "Excel & Google Sheets template" in line 2 reinforces discoverability without keyword stuffing.

**Specificity kills vagueness**: "color-coded milestones" and "10 goals simultaneously" are concrete vs. generic "milestone tracking."

**Buyer psychology**: "tired of guessing" addresses frustration. "See exactly where you stand" promises clarity. People buy solutions to feelings, not features.

**Readability**: Shorter sentences. Each line delivers one complete thought. Easier to skim on mobile where 80% of Etsy traffic happens.

The original wasn't *bad*—it just assumed people already wanted the product. The rewrite meets them earlier in the decision process when they're still figuring out what solution they need.
