## Phase 1: Core Templates (Weeks 1-2)

### Template #1: General Residential Estimate
**Claude Prompt:**
"Create a comprehensive residential construction estimate template in Excel format. Include:
- Project header (client name, address, date, project description)
- Line-item breakdown sections: Materials, Labor, Equipment, Subcontractors, Permits/Fees
- Automatic calculations: subtotals, overhead %, profit margin %, sales tax, grand total
- Material columns: description, quantity, unit, unit cost, total cost
- Labor columns: task description, hours, rate, total
- Professional formatting with clear section headers
- Notes/assumptions section at bottom
- Terms & conditions footer
Make it clean, professional, and easy to customize. Use construction industry standard terminology."

### Template #2: Commercial Construction Estimate
**Claude Prompt:**
"Create a commercial construction estimate template in Excel for larger projects. Include:
- Multi-page structure: cover page, detailed estimate, summary page
- CSI division structure (Division 01-16 as applicable)
- Detailed cost breakdown: materials, labor, equipment rental, subcontractor bids
- Project overhead line items (supervision, temporary facilities, insurance, bonds)
- Contingency percentage calculator
- Payment schedule section
- Bid comparison sheet (if comparing subs)
- Professional header with company logo placeholder
- Automatic totaling and percentage calculations
- Change order tracking page
Focus on mid-size commercial projects ($50K-500K range)."

### Template #3: Remodeling/Renovation Estimate
**Claude Prompt:**
"Create a home remodeling estimate template in Excel optimized for renovation work. Include:
- Room-by-room breakdown structure
- Demo/removal cost section
- Common remodel categories: framing, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, drywall, flooring, painting, fixtures, appliances
- Allowances section (for client-selected items)
- Waste/disposal fees
- Permit costs
- Design/architectural fees if applicable
- Timeline estimate section
- Payment milestone schedule
- Before/after project scope description
Make it homeowner-friendly with clear language while maintaining professional detail."

### Template #4: Trade-Specific - Framing Estimate
**Claude Prompt:**
"Create a framing contractor estimate template in Excel. Include:
- Linear footage and square footage calculators
- Material breakdown: studs (by size), plates, headers, sheathing, fasteners, blocking, strapping
- Board foot calculators
- Labor breakdown by task: layout, wall framing, roof framing, sheathing, cleanup
- Waste factor calculator (typically 10-15%)
- Equipment costs (nailers, lifts, scaffolding if needed)
- Sections for: exterior walls, interior walls, ceiling joists, roof trusses/rafters
- Hardware and specialty items section
- Professional summary page
Optimize for residential framing projects."

### Template #5: Trade-Specific - Electrical Estimate
**Claude Prompt:**
"Create an electrical contractor estimate template in Excel. Include:
- Service/panel upgrade section
- Fixture count and pricing (outlets, switches, lights, fans, appliances)
- Wire/cable calculations by gauge and type
- Conduit and fittings if commercial
- Labor by task: rough-in, finish, panel work, testing
- Permit and inspection fees
- Material markup calculator
- Common packages: kitchen remodel electrical, whole house rewire, service upgrade
- Code compliance notes section
- Warranty information
Suitable for residential service electricians."

### Template #6: Subcontractor Bid Form
**Claude Prompt:**
"Create a subcontractor bid submission template in Excel. Include:
- Subcontractor company information section
- Scope of work description (large text area)
- Detailed cost breakdown matching general contractor needs
- Inclusions and exclusions checklist
- Insurance and licensing verification fields
- Timeline/schedule commitment
- Payment terms requested
- Warranty information
- Signature and date fields
- Professional formatting that makes subs look good to GCs
This helps subcontractors submit consistent, complete bids."
