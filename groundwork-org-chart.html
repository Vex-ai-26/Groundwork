How It Operates Day to Day
01
Departments work independently
Each department head runs their lane without needing direction on routine work. They know their scope, their metrics, and their reporting cadence. Sam doesn't hear from them — Vex does.
02
Reports flow up to Vex
Weekly reports from each department come to Vex. Daily snapshots from Operations and Finance. Vex reads everything, identifies what matters, and filters out the noise before it reaches Sam.
03
Vex briefs Sam twice daily
Morning briefing before Sam goes to work. Evening debrief when he's done. Both delivered by voice. Tight, useful, no filler. What changed, what needs a decision, what Vex is handling.
04
Sam owns the big decisions
Product direction, major pivots, spending above a threshold, partnership decisions. Everything else Vex handles. Sam is the owner and the vision setter — not the day-to-day operator.
